From Garden To Plate

A potager is a French term for an ornamental vegetable or kitchen garden. Often flowers (edible and non-edible) and herbs are planted with the vegetable to enchance the garden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to plant

Plant are chosen as much for their functionality as for their color and form. Many are trained to grow upward. A well designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can desiguised their function of providing for a home in a wide array of forms - from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formality of the knot garden.
