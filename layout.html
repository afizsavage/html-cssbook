Fixed Gear Forever

16 APRIL 2020

The veloheld combines minimalist designs with suberb quality. Devoid of excessive graphics and gear shift components, the veloheld product range delight us with its beauty and simplicity. The black and white (yin and yang?) bicycles feature a short wheelbase, a single gear and a narrow handlebar... All you need to explore the city streets..

For those who wants to create their bikes themselves, the veloheld frames come in three sizes and two colors and are the perfect starting point.
